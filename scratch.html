Why these interests matter to me

Basketball and off-road builds both teach the same thing: progress comes from consistency. In basketball, that means reps, discipline, and effort. In building a vehicle, that means planning, patience, and learning how small upgrades can change performance and style.

This page combines both interests while showing required HTML features like lists, anchors, images, video, div layout, and a custom stylesheet.

My progression list

  1. Basketball goals
    • Improve conditioning
    • Develop a better shooting routine
    • Become more explosive off the dribble
  2. 4Runner goals
    • Add a more aggressive stance
    • Upgrade suspension and tires
    • Make it cleaner, more unique, and more functional
  3. Mindset goals
    • Stay disciplined with school and work
    • Keep building practical skills
    • Use challenges as chances to improve
